2.Project:
Smart OPD System

Member Name:Isha Tundurwar

I developed 'Smart OPD System' which is a is a digital healthcare solution designed to simplify and improve the outpatient department (OPD) process in hospitals. It allows patients to book appointments online, reducing long waiting times and overcrowding in hospitals. The system provides features like doctor scheduling, patient registration, and real-time appointment tracking, making the process more organized and efficient. Patients receive notifications and reminders, which helps them manage their visits better. On the hospital side, it includes a doctor dashboard and admin panel to manage patient data, appointments, and daily schedules effectively. This system enhances the overall patient experience while improving hospital workflow and efficiency.

Takeaways : Smart OPD System is an online platform that streamlines hospital appointments, reduces waiting time, and improves patient and doctor interaction.
